Thruster make your ship move. If your ship ship doesn't have any thrusters, then it can't be spawned. Speed is measured in meters/second. Speed is calculated as follows: [speed] = 9 * [total thrust] / [total mass]. Turn speed is measured in degrees per second. The turn speed of your ship is calculated as follows: total turn speed = ([total part TS] * 15.9893 + 916.8) / [total mass].
